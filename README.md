Privacy Policy for Counted – Personal Logbook

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Counted – Personal Logbook is designed to be simple, private, and respectful of your data.
Anything you log is personal, and we believe it should remain fully under your control.

This Privacy Policy explains how Counted handles your data.

Information We Collect

Counted does not collect personal data.

All information you enter into the app — such as logs, counts, and logbook names — is stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not:

- Collect personal information
- Require accounts or sign-ups
- Track usage or behavior
- Collect analytics or diagnostic data
- Store logbook data externally
- Use third-party analytics or tracking services
- Create user profiles
- Share data with third parties

Local Device Storage

All app data, including:

- Your logbooks
- Logged entries and counts
- App preferences

is stored only on your device.

This data is never uploaded, synced, or shared.
If you uninstall the app, all stored data is permanently deleted.

App Permissions

Counted may request the following permission:

- Notifications (optional):
Used only to send reminders if you choose to enable them.

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and not for data collection or tracking.

Data Security

Because all data remains on your device:

- No data is transmitted over the internet
- No external backups are created by us
- Your logbook remains private and fully under your control

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date above.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Use Counted without data collection
- Keep all logbook data private and local
- Delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app
